About Project

non-profit web application

This non-profit web application main aim is to allow public of all age groups to donate clothes and money to the less privileged. This is to raise awarness about poverty which is often ignores as we live in a city with easy access to basic necessities and education.In fact, it is worth noting that Singapore has the 2nd largest income disparity and it is also one of the wealthiest country in the world.Hope, that this wesbite will expose the upcoming youths to the habit of donation which is not necessarily monetary means and as the future generation they have the ability to make a change to the society.
