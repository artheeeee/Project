Donation Information

Cash Donation

Spend as less as a DOLLAR to help others feel happy :)

You are doing a huge role in helping to reduce poverty by donating less than 1% of your income. You will be helping the people in need of the three most essential needs in life "FOOD,EDUCATION & SHELTER". We will be 100% donating the amount to the respective option selection you have made.

  • Food Donation starts from $1(1 Meal) to $7(7 Meals)
  • Education Donation starts from $10 : (1 year stationary+books supplies == 1 Child)
  • Grocery Donation starts from $15 : (rice bags, vegetables, hygiene products = 1 Family)
  • Special Day Donation starts from $30 : Is it your birthday? anniversary? special day? and feel like doing a extra special deed to make others feel happy. This is a great option to help a community by getting them food!

Donation Box Subscription

Do not own a credit card but want to help? Fret Not! This option is made JUST FOR YOU!

Donation Box is similar to your piggy bank that you used to have when you were young to save up some cash. Similarly, you will be saving up some cash as little as you can and make the donation after the subscription period is over as penny simple as that! Steps to note:

  • Step 1: Fill up the subscription form
  • Step 2: Choose your desired subscription months or years
  • Step 3: Fill up the donation box to the brim ;)
  • Step 4: Return to the nearest community centre after done!

Envelope Subscription

Feeling UNSAFE to make an online donation? or want to be NOSTALGIC in making a donation?

Envelope subscription is specially catered to our prestigious eldely community out there, Don't feel left out as this subscription plan has got you covered. You just have to subscrie to this donation scheme and you will receive an envelope right to your doorstep to make the donation and all you have to do is : SEAL IT, POST IT and get NOTIFIED! You will be notified through an email/sms after your donation envelope has reached us!

Clothing Subscription

Option OTHER THAN MONEY, do not feel burdened by needing to use cash for donation simply switch to NONMONETARY donation!

Non-Monetary donation option for you! Anyone of Any age regardless of gender will be able to help out in this donation. Feeling guilty that your spending at least 80% of your income on clothes? or Upset that the clothes do not fit you anymore? Just DONATE them! These clothes that are being donated will be directly passed on to the rural poverty places for people who might really need them. Steps to note:

  • Step 1: Fill up the clothing donation form
  • Step 2: Gather your old clothes in GOOD CONDITION
  • Step 3: Donate it at the nearest Community Centre
